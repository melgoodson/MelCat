Strategy Plan


1) Goal

Launch Snarky Cat™ as:
  1. a standalone digital product generating incremental revenue
  2. a bundle enhancer that increases AOV and repeat purchase across the current product line

2) Core Product Thesis

A character alone is brand content.
A character asset pack is a product people will pay for because it delivers:
  • utility (reactions, stickers, meme templates)
  • identity (brand voice they want to “wear”)
  • shareability (built to spread through DMs and Stories)

3) Offer Architecture


Lite Pack (Free)
  • Purpose: distribution + email capture + adoption
  • Contents: 6 GIFs + 6 static stickers + simple install instructions

Standard Pack ($12.99–$14.99)
  • Purpose: impulse purchase, broad appeal
  • Contents: 20–40 GIFs + 20 stickers + wallpapers + meme templates + printable cards

Deluxe Pack ($24.99)
  • Purpose: monetize superfans
  • Adds: expanded animations + seasonal Drop #1 + exclusive variants

Ultimate ($39.99)
  • Purpose: lock in LTV
  • Adds: 6-month drop pass / ongoing updates

4) Funnel & Customer Journey


Path A: Standalone
  • Social/email → landing page → product preview → checkout
  • Post-purchase: “Upgrade to Deluxe” + “Share this pack” prompt

Path B: Physical Bundle Flywheel
  • Physical purchase → QR unlock (Standard free or discounted)
  • Upsell: Deluxe + future drops
    Follow-up: UGC prompt (“Caption This Cat”) + referral incentive

5) Distribution Strategy


Owned: site, email, SMS, inserts, post-purchase flows
Viral: GIPHY/Tenor + reaction GIF search placement
UGC loop: weekly template prompt + feature winners + store credit prizes

6) Validation Plan (Pre-Production)

  • Fake-door checkout A/B test at $12.99 vs $14.99 vs $19.99
  • Waitlist gate to measure demand without overbuilding
  • Style vote (3 directions) to ensure audience alignment

Go/no-go criteria
  • strong buy-intent clicks
  • meaningful waitlist sign-ups
  • clear preference for a single style direction

7) Production System

Deliverables:
  • Character bible (voice rules, visual rules, catchphrase bank)
  • Reaction list (standardized set: eye-roll, “nah,” judgment stare, etc.)
  • Asset formats: GIF/MP4, PNG sticker sheets, wallpapers, PDF printables
  • Installation & usage guide

8) Success Metrics

Revenue: conversion rate, attach rate, upgrade rate, AOV lift
Brand: GIF usage volume, UGC volume, list growth, returning buyers

9) Risk Management

  • Ensure “use it in 5 minutes” onboarding
  • Over-index on previews and clear “what’s inside”
  • Maintain drop cadence to prevent novelty decay

Baseline success probability: ~58% (if sold as “a character”)
Improved probability: 75–85% (if executed as a utility pack + bundle funnel + external distribution + drops)
